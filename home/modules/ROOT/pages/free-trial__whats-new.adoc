<h3 id="password-creation-checklist"><a class="anchor" href="#password-creation-checklist"></a>Password creation checklist</h3>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>We redesigned the account creation page to clarify the password requirements for new users.
Now, when you first log in to Free Trial, the password verification checklist appears.
Your password must meet the following requirements:</p>
</div>
<div class="ulist">
<ul>
<li>
<p>The password must be 8 characters or more in length.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The password must include at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number, and 1 special character.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The password must pass a complexity test based on an external library.
This test ensures password complexity and uniqueness by checking for known patterns or words that are too simple.
If the password is not complex enough, ThoughtSpot rejects it, even if it fulfills the other requirements.
Refer to the <a href="https://github.com/dropbox/zxcvbn">Dropbox password library</a> for more information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The password cannot use certain blocked words.
By default, the blocked words are: your username and any part of your display name.</p>
<div class="paragraph">
<p><span class="image"><img src="_images/free-trial-password-checklist.png" alt="free trial password checklist"></span></p>
</div>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="sect2">
<h3 id="worksheet-creation-tooltips"><a class="anchor" href="#worksheet-creation-tooltips"></a>Worksheet creation tooltips in Setup</h3>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>To simplify the process of creating worksheets on Free Trial, we added tooltips to explain the difference between fact tables and dimension tables.
When creating a worksheet, you first select the fact table that forms the base of your business use case, then add dimension tables with common dimensions to your fact table.
For example, to create a Sales worksheet, you join together a sales fact table with dimension tables that share common dimensions like date, region, and store.</p>
</div>
<div class="paragraph">
<p>The worksheet creation tooltips appear when you create a worksheet during onboarding.
To view examples of typical fact tables, proceed to step one of the worksheet creation process in Setup, and click the information icon to the right of the <strong>Select a fact table</strong> dialog.
To view examples of typical dimension tables, click the information icon to the right of the <strong>Select up to 4 dimension tables</strong> dialog.</p>
</div>
